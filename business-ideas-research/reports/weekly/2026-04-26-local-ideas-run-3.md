## Best first bet

The strongest first test is **AI-диспетчер заявок для домашних услуг**. It is broad enough to find customers quickly, but the first version should be narrow:

- one vertical first, probably cleaning, HVAC, plumbing, or appliance repair;
- missed-call text-back before full voice AI;
- short intake flow: problem, address, urgency, photos, preferred time;
- owner notification in Telegram/WhatsApp;
- simple dashboard showing recovered leads.

## Why not start with dentistry

Dentistry has high value, but it is crowded in 2026 and has more trust/regulatory friction. It remains a watch idea, not the first launch.
