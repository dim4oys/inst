# Idea: AI-агент лицензий и разрешений

## Snapshot

- Status: watch
- Date discovered: 2026-04-26
- Market: US, EU
- Source count: 6
- Score: 36/50

## Problem

Малые бизнесы пропускают renewals, путают activity codes, теряют документы and pay penalties. Simple reminder tools help, but they do not prepare renewal packets.

## Proposed business

AI reads old permits, renewal notices and local agency instructions; prepares a checklist and draft packet for human review.

## Why It Is Less Crowded

Reminder tools exist. The gap is packet preparation by region and business type.
